ETS

Malaysia's ETS, operated by Keretapi Tanah Melayu Berhad, is the country's fastest inter-city rail, reaching speeds of up to 140 km/h (87 mph). It runs between Gemas and Padang Besar near the Malaysia-Thai border.

KTM

KTM Komuter: Malaysia's commuter rail, launched in 1995, serves Kuala Lumpur and nearby areas. Expanded to northern states in 2015, it offers air-conditioned trains and 'Park & Ride' facilities, providing convenient travel for urban and suburban commuters.
